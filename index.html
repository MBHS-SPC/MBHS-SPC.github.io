Speed Trig

Speed Trig is an application designed to help users improve their ability to compute basic trigonometric functions by practicing with digital quizzes on the unit circle. You can visit our Speed Trig Android Repository or our Speed Trig iOS Repository. The app can be found on the Android play store

Blair Talks

Blair Talks allows people to see their favorite teachers and hear their catchphrases. You can visit our Blair Talks Android Repository or our Blair Talks iOS Repository. The app can be found on the Android Play store and the Apple App Store
