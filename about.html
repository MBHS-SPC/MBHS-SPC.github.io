Smartphone Programming Club

About Us

We're the Smartphone Programming Club from Montgomery Blair High School. We create apps for our own enjoyment, as well as for various groups around the school. If you'd like to learn how to create apps for Android and iOS, then you should come to our meetings (every other Friday in room 314)! If you've got an idea for an app you think we should make, feel free to get in touch with us on the contact page.
